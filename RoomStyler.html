Redesign a Space Easily With RoomStyler!

RoomStyler is an App that allows you to scan a room with precise measurements of furniture and other objects so you can rearrange virtually before having to lift a finger. Except for the one you’ll use to download the app.

We saw a need for young designers and movers to have a an app that can aid them in rearranging, moving and designing a space.

Role

UX, UI Designer

Team size: 3 Designers

In this research project, my team and I planned and conducted 6 interviews , 7 survey responses with potential users as well as 2 client stakeholder evaluations.

As researchers we want to understand

  • What tools or methods people use when moving furniture.
  • What issues arise during rearranging or moving furniture and other home decor items.
  • How people feel about the options available to them regarding redesigning a room or moving into a new space.

    • User Research

    During our interview phase, we discovered that creative people need easier ways of rearranging a space, therefore we believe that creating an app that utilizes the measure tool on IOS and Quick Measure on Android, lets the user freely decorate their space before doing any heavy lifting. Doing this will allow our users to save time and energy before rearranging or moving into a space.

    Problem Statement

    RoomStyler was designed to help users rearrange their space before lifting a finger to move heavy furniture. We have observed that some apps don’t currently offer this service and not meeting user expectations of precise measurements around items in it's space. How might we improve RoomStyler so that our customers are successful in having a stress free moving experience.

    We brainstormed features and ideas using the I Like, I Wish, What If mind mapping tool. We found that users like to have creative freedom, wish there was an auto measuring tool, and wondering what it would be like if you can magically move furniture around.

    As a result from our ideation phase, we placed the most ideal features in each box based on the level of complexity and priority. As a result, the three key features we chose were mapping out a room/space, using your camera to take photo of objects, and being able to rearrange a room using AR.
